There is a clear need for students when it comes to finding a desired
extension from a specific department. Since students and university faculty
members donot possess a public online catalogue for extensions, they waste
much of their time searching for outdated extensions on the internet. Theres
a need for outsiders to have the extensions easily available. If an
employee from Google wants to give a workshop in the university, they need
to have at hand the extensions available, so they can coordinate which
specific classroom the workshop is going to be given in.
