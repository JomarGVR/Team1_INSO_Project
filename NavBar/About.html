What we provide:

  1. Facilitate getting the extensions and the process of calls and visits to the specific offices.
  2. Provide a Q & A service in order for students to find the appropriate extension.
  3. Provide a Forum so students can ask questions to be guided to the appropriate extension.
  4. Prevent agglomerations of phone calls that are not supposed to be in that extension or are there by mistake.
  5. Helps the user to prevent time loss while searching up the extensions.
  6. Provide an email feedback form option where the user can provide feedback and start initiatives for the site.
